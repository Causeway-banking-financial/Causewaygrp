# Research: Amortization Schedule & WhatsApp Integration

## Islamic Finance Amortization Best Practices

### Key Components for Murabaha/Ijara Amortization Schedule:
1. **Payment Number** - Sequential numbering (1, 2, 3...)
2. **Payment Date** - Both Gregorian and Hijri dates
3. **Beginning Balance** - Outstanding principal at start of period
4. **Payment Amount** - Fixed monthly installment
5. **Profit Portion** - Calculated based on outstanding balance Ã— rate
6. **Principal Portion** - Payment minus profit portion
7. **Ending Balance** - Beginning balance minus principal portion
8. **Cumulative Profit Paid** - Running total of profit payments
9. **Cumulative Principal Paid** - Running total of principal payments

### AAOIFI Disclosure Requirements:
- Clear separation of profit vs principal
- Total profit over life of contract
- Effective profit rate disclosure
- Early settlement terms
- Penalty-free prepayment options (Sharia requirement)

### Professional Formatting Standards:
- Alternating row colors for readability
- Bold headers with clear labels
- Currency formatting with proper separators
- Percentage formatting for rates
- Summary totals at bottom
- Page breaks for multi-page schedules

1. **Position**: Bottom-right corner (standard UX convention)
2. **Size**: 50-60px diameter for mobile touch targets
3. **Animation**: Subtle pulse or bounce to draw attention
4. **Color**: WhatsApp green (#25D366) for instant recognition
5. **Tooltip**: Show on hover "Chat with us on WhatsApp"
