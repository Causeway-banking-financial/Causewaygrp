# CauseWay Academy - Training Programs Research

## From Company Profile - Core Service Areas:

### 1. Islamic Finance Engineering & Sharia Governance
- Product line architecture (not isolated products)
- Sharia control-point mapping embedded into workflows
- SSB briefing packs (fatwa-ready documentation support)
- Sharia audit traceability standards and evidence files
- Training for Sharia compliance and operations teams

### 2. Financial Product Development
- Donor/NGO contract finance and supplier ecosystems
- SME, agriculture and value-chain finance
- Trade and import finance (Sharia-compliant structures)
- Renewable and solar finance
- Partnership modes (Musharakah/Mudarabah) with clear governance

### 3. Wealth & Investment / Treasury Governance
- Investment policies (IPS), mandates, limits, governance design
- Portfolio architecture (liquidity tiers; short-cycle vs longer-cycle)
- Treasury committee flows, reporting and escalation rules
- Sharia investment governance (screening logic, audit pathways)
- Board-grade performance and risk reporting

### 4. Risk, Compliance, and Audit Readiness
- AML/CFT frameworks: KYC/CDD, monitoring, reporting, governance
- Sanctions screening governance and exception handling logic
- Control matrices, SOPs, training packs, audit trail standards
- Credit governance and portfolio risk policies

### 5. Institutional Transformation
- Governance uplift, committee architecture, delegations of authority
- Operating model design (HQ vs branches, process ownership)
- Product portfolio modernization into governed product lines
- Process re-engineering (BPR) and bottleneck removal
- Segregation of duties, maker/checker controls
