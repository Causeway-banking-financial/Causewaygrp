# CauseWay Website Quality Audit Report
## Benchmark: McKinsey, Deloitte, PwC, BCG Standards

---

## Executive Summary

This audit evaluates the CauseWay website against global consulting firm standards (McKinsey, Deloitte, PwC, BCG) and identifies areas for improvement to achieve world-class quality.

---

## Quality Benchmarks Applied

### 1. Visual Design Standards
- **Typography**: Clean, professional font hierarchy
- **Color Consistency**: Brand colors used systematically
- **Whitespace**: Generous spacing for premium feel
- **Imagery**: High-quality, relevant visuals

### 2. User Experience Standards
- **Navigation**: Clear, intuitive site structure
- **Mobile Responsiveness**: Flawless mobile experience
- **Load Performance**: Fast page loads
- **Accessibility**: WCAG 2.1 compliance

### 3. Content Standards
- **Professional Tone**: Authoritative, precise language
- **Bilingual Quality**: Equal quality in Arabic/English
- **Value Proposition**: Clear differentiation
